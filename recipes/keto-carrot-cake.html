Keto Carrot Cake

A desert that will make you blush!

Oh what a good day it is. Here is a carrott cake you could (almost) completely indulge with.

It's yummy, have a just enough sweet taste andonly contains 3g of carbs per slice

Ingredients

  • Stevia
  • Almond Flour
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Baking powder
  • Cinnamon
  • Sea Salt
  • Shredded Carrots
  • Chopped Pecans
  • Cream Cheese

Steps

  • Cream butter and sweetener
  • Mix dry ingredients
  • Add the grated carrots and pecans
  • Spoon the batter into the prepared baking pans and smooth the top
  • Bake
  • Make frosting
  • Assemble
